“Blindenschrift”, Waggonhalle Marburg, 2011

Director: Mareike Nele Dobewall, Scenography: Mareike Nele Dobewall, Actors: Anna Dassler and Ingrun Hillesheim

World premiere of Lars Poppes short drama “Blindenschrift” at Marburger Kurzdramenwettbewerb.

Two characters are stuck with each other and in their roles. Familiar and shared games help pass the time but also reveal the monotony of their lives. What is friendship if there is only one more person around?
