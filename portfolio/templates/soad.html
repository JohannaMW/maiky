“Story of a Dress”, 2012

Director: Mareike Nele Dobewall
Costume: Malin Nilsen
Camera: Pål Ulvik Rokseth
Sound:Jacob Dobewall
Music: Markus Hernes
Make-Up: Anna Filosa

The journey of the dress starts in the universe of materials. Through contact with the human body it takes shape. It becomes alive through the movement of the person. Then it can show all its characteristics. There is a relationship between a dress and the person who wears it. There is freedom and restriction. The dress changes its shape in different places. Until it disappears into nature.

Story of a Dress from Mareike Dobewall.
