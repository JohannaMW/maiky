„Two People“

Written, produced
and directed by Mareike Dobewall

Catering: Brigitte Goroncy

Inspired by Edward Hoppers painting “Excursion into Philosophy“ this film is about two lonely people who are unable to escape from the monotony of their lives. Unable to communicate with each other they spend a moment of tenderness which leaves them with an even deeper feeling of emptiness.

Two People from Mareike Dobewall.
