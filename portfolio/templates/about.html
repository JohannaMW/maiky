My motivation
I am fascinated by the hidden potential of people, spaces and situations. Curiosity for what someone or something withholds that I can trigger and tease to come out is the main drive in my work. When uncovered potentials start to reveal I work on making it sensible for an audience by creating a relationship between them. In my work I am constantly looking for new possibilities to reach others, to find a way to move them. For me it is the most valid reason for the existence of art that it can get people to have empathy with other people, themselves (through reflection), or even things.

Film and Scenography
In my films I have always worked on giving space for feelings and thoughts by drawing the audience into a story inside an atmosphere of sound, light, shadow and colors. Now as a scenographer I put a main focus on creating sensational experiences in order to stimulate the above mentioned. Herein I am interested in dealing with the so called public space and to create big spacial interventions.

Directing Theatre
In the past years I have worked as a theatre director and developed a very physical form of theatre making. Captivating the audience with the power of the actor, a precise choreography and the strength of a visual and audible composition is the aim. - A world where a dialogue between the seen and what happens inside of us while we watch a piece is possible.

Photography
In my photography I explore mood, shapes and textures and how they speak to a person who meets that image. In relation to film I am fascinated how one frame, a still, can pull you deeper in.

Opera
My fascination for opera arose two years ago when I created an aria performance in the building opposite of Folkoperan in Stockholm. The result was a „Street Opera“ that gave the general public access to opera music. Opera revealed for me the huge potential it has to touch people and to display the interior of what it means to be human. I am now interested in varying the distance between the musicians and the audience and, in consequence, to increase the impact the music, the voice of the singer, the presence of everybody in the space and the space has on the experience of an opera piece.
