“Memorial for 800” by Håøya in Norway 2014 (NTA)

by Mareike Nele Dobewall, Freya Sif Hestnes and Camilla Haegebarth,
Tutor: Serge von Arx (NTA)

For the memorial for the 800 young german soldiers that died in the Second World War in the fjord in Norway we collected sticks on location, all individual in their appearance, and put them into a different context. We painted them white and gave each of them a number in order to give a possibility for the finder to connect. The frame was set clearly: At the time the Kiel-Oslo ferry would pass above the wrack of the „Blücher“ we would set the 800 sticks free, into the water. How the memorial would evolve was kept open.
